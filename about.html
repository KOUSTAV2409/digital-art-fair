Digital Art Fair, founded in 2020, is the world's first Web 3.0 Fine Art Fair with a focus on innovative art created for every demographic that wishes to appreciate, create, and collect digital and NFT Fine Art. With the vision to cultivate and inspire a new generation of art collectors to embrace the use of digital technology to explore the beauty of art and culture.

THE VISION

Digital Art Fair specialises in the intersection of art and technology, showcasing the wide breadth of digital fine art & NFT. The popular rise of digital art & NFTs has changed how art is being consumed in the digital era. At Digital Art Fair we merge and celebrate all forms of digital art with physical, tangible event experiences.

Embracing new technologies and focusing on digital fine art & NFTS- we provide a unique platform to experience, educate and inspire.

Get inspired by cutting-edge technologies such as animation, moving image, video art, AI (artificial intelligence), VR (virtual reality), AR (augmented reality), 360-immersive room, digital projection mapping, and NFT blockchain technology.

DIGITAL ART FAIR

FOUNDATION

Digital Art Fair is the leading Web 3.0 annual art fair in the world of the digital art era. Showcasing some of the most innovative and exciting digital art and NFTs from around the globe. The fair provides a platform for emerging and established artists to exhibit their work, and for art enthusiasts and collectors to discover and acquire new digital artwork. With the vision to cultivate and inspire a new generation of art collectors to embrace the use of digital technology to explore the beauty of art and culture.
